Miso Soup

Description

Miso soup is a traditional Japanese soup consisting of a stock called "dashi" into which softened miso paste is mixed. Many ingredients are added depending on regional and seasonal recipes, and personal preference.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup miso paste
  • 4 cups water
  • 1/2 cup cubed tofu
  • 1/2 cup sliced fresh mushrooms
  • 1/2 cup chopped green onions
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 sheet nori seaweed, cut into thin strips

Steps

  1. Bring water to a boil in a large saucepan. Add miso paste, tofu, mushrooms, and green onions. Reduce heat to medium, and simmer gently for 5 minutes. Add soy sauce. Ladle into bowls, and garnish with nori.
