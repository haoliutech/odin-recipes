Yakitori

Description

Chicken yakitori is an easy Japanese dish to make at home. Serve with white rice and sake for an easy but special meal!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup mirin
  • 1/2 cup sake
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1 pound boneless skinless chicken thighs
  • 1 bunch green onions
  • 1 tablespoon neutral oil
  • 1 tablespoon toasted sesame seeds

Steps

  1. Combine soy sauce, mirin, sake, and sugar in a small saucepan and bring to a boil over high heat. Reduce heat to low and simmer until slightly thickened, about 15 minutes. Remove from heat and let cool.
  2. Cut chicken thighs into 1-inch pieces. Cut white and light green parts of green onions into 1-inch pieces. Cut dark green parts of green onions into thin slices.
  3. Thread chicken and green onions onto skewers, alternating between chicken and green onions.
  4. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add skewers and cook until browned on all sides, about 2 minutes per side. Add sauce and bring to a boil. Reduce heat to low and simmer until chicken is cooked through, about 5 minutes. Sprinkle with sesame seeds and dark green parts of green onions.
